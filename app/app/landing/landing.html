  • How We Met

    2012: Sophia and Richard met while doing their undergrad degrees at McGill. They fell in love through their time there and have been together ever since! They have made it through living in separate countries, a pandemic and Richard's love of the Bruins!!

  • The Engagement

    August 2025: Sophia and Richard decided to get married after 9 years together. They discussed it together, as they do everything and decided that this was something they both wanted. After they decided they called their families and told them, much to their excitement!

  • The Wedding

    September 20, 2025: Sophia and Richard discovered Mount Auburn Cemetery through one of their many death marches they do for fun! This beautiful green space seemed like the perfect place for their wedding and they are excited to get married there.

    • Venue Details
      • The ceremony will be held indoors in the Bigelow Chapel. The Bigelow Chapel is located in the Mount Auburn Cemetery.
      • The reception will be held indoors.
      • Free parking is available at both ceremony and reception venues.
      • Average temperatures in December: High of 58°F (14°C), Low of 34°F (1°C).
    • Attire & Etiquette
      • Dress code is semi-formal to formal.
      • Please dress accordingly for the ceremony, as it will be outdoors.
      • The ceremony will start promptly as scheduled. Please arrive on time.
      • Personal photography is allowed. However, please be courteous and do not hinder the wedding photographer.

      • A limited number of rooms are available through our room block at the Flamingo. Reservations can be made online or by calling the reservations department at 888-373-9855. Please mention group code VF3TCK6 and ask for the "Tiffany Chau and Kelvin Dealca Wedding" room block. More details.
