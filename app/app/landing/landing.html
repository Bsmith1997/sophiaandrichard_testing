  • How We Met

    2012: Tiffany and Kelvin were coworkers on the same team. Kelvin made the first move. The first date consisted of a lakeside French dinner at Marche Bacchus, ice skating at The Cosmopolitan, and watching 'Blades of Glory' movie (to continue the night's ice skating theme).

  • The Proposal

    January 9, 2016: Kelvin took Tiffany out to dinner at Raku for their third anniversary, and planned for dessert back at home with a surprise. Tiffany unfortunately ate too much during dinner, and delayed having dessert for a couple of hours. The anxious but patient Kelvin eventually popped the question with a Sprinkles cupcake paired with the stellar ring.

  • The Wedding

    December 18, 2016: The Flamingo Hotel & Casino is sentimental, as it is where Tiffany and Kelvin first met and worked together. Tiffany also likes to watch the turtles at the Flamingo's Wildlife Habitat. The Sparklings is an exquisite restaurant that hosted many of Tiffany's family's events and gatherings over the years.
