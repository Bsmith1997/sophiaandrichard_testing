    Welcome to Somerville

    • Venue Details
      • The ceremony will be held indoors in the Bigelow Chapel. The Bigelow Chapel is located in the Mount Auburn Cemetery. Photos will be taken outside, weather permitting.
      • The reception will be held indoors.
      • Parking is limited at the venue and there is no parking at the reception. We recommend you walk, or Uber/taxi to get there.
      • Average temperatures in September: High of 25°C, Low of 15°C.
    • Attire & Etiquette
      • Dress code is cocktail attire.
      • The ceremony will start promptly as scheduled. Please arrive on time.
      • Personal photography is allowed. However, please be courteous and do not hinder the wedding photographer.
    • Accommodation & Transportation
      • A limited number of rooms are available through our room block at local hotels. Updates to come!
      • We do not recommend driving yourself in the Boston area. Public Transit, Taxi, Uber, or Lyft services are available.
